Hello! My name is Alex Eversbusch and I am a Musician, Landscape Architect, and aspiring Developer. I have been studying and practicing web development since July of 2018, but I have been passionate about creating things since I was very young.

I started playing the drums when I was 10, and writing my own music when I was 12. I have not been able to turn off the creative side of my brain since! For the last four years since graduating from the University of Georga with a bachelor of Landscape Architecture I have been practicing both residential and commercial Landscape Architecture in Atlanta.

I am now in a full-stack development bootcamp at Georgia Tech, and I am loving it! Head on over to my portfolio for some examples of my work from the bootcamp, as well as some of the musical projects I have been involved in over the years!
